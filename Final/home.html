Cat Safety

The project lets you see some of the dangers that meet cats outdoors and that they cause. The viewer can use the WASD keys to move around.

Dangers
Artist Statement

The work is playing with what is dangerous for a small creature such as a cat in the modern world. The 3D figures are made in blender with a simple feel. The controllable land of the project that holds everything and lets the viewer move is A-Frame. The A-Frame lets everything be laid out for the viewer to look around. The viewer can go left or right from the starting point and see a few of the outdoor dangers in a space. The Cat is known for their curiously for a reason which can lead them to trouble.
